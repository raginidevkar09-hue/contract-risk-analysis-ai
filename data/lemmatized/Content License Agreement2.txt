 1 purpose of the agreement 
  1.1 the purpose of this content distribution agreement ( hereinafter 
  " agreement " ) be to set forth the term and condition under which 
  plan_b may use the content ( " content " as set forth in appendix 2 ) 
  own or license by licensor for a commercial distribution to 
  plan_b 's end user in the territory ( " territory " as set forth in 
  appendix 2 ) . 
  1.2 end user mean any third party receive content on a mobile 
  device for a payment in accordance with the term and condition of 
  this agreement for their own private and non- commercial use . 
 2 obligation of licensor 
  2.1 licensor shall make a first delivery of content to plan_b within 
  14 day after the signing of this agreement unless separately agree 
  between the party . 
  2.2 licensor shall deliver content accord to the specification 
  ( for example format , file size ) set by plan_b or to be agree 
  between the party in writing . 
  2.3 licensor grant plan_b for the term of this agreement the right 
  to produce , market and distribute content to end user ( in the 
  territory specify in appendix 2 ) through its own and its partner 's 
  platform . 
 3 obligation of plan_b 
  3.1 plan_b will distribute content to end user in the territory 
  through its distribution channel . 
  3.2 plan_b shall use reasonable commercial effort to market and 
  stimulate interest in the content with its customer . 
  3.3 plan_b shall provide licensor with a detailed write record , 
  which include the number of end user download and each distribution 
  channel . such report shall be provide to licensor in electronic 
  format within 6 week of the end of a quarter . 
  3.4 licensor shall have the right to use a certify public 
  accountant to inspect and audit all the relate record and book of 
  plan_b to ensure plan_b 's compliance with the term of this agreement . 
  in the event that any such audit reveal that plan_b have underpaid 
  fee to the value of ten ( 10 ) percent or more of the total amount of 
  payment for the period cover by the audit , plan_b shall bear the 
  cost of the audit and shall in any event immediately pay to licensor

 6 confidentiality 
  6.1 each party shall keep in confidence all material and information , 
  include without limitation content , receive from the other party 
  and mark as confidential or which should be understand to be 
  confidential , and may not use such material or information for any 
  other purpose than those set forth in this agreement . the 
  confidentiality obligation shall , however , not be apply to material 
  and information , which as show by the receive party , 
  6.1.1 be generally available or otherwise public; or 
  6.1.2 the receive party have receive from a third party without 
  any obligation of confidentiality; or 
  6.1.3 be in the possession of the receive party prior to 
  receipt of the same from the other party without any obligation 
  of confidentiality relate thereto; or 
  6.1.4 the receive party have independently develop without 
  use material or information receive from the other party . 
  6.2 each party shall promptly upon termination of this agreement or 
  when the party no long need the material or information in question 
  for the purpose state in this agreement cease use confidential 
  material and information receive from the other party and , unless the 
  party separately agree on destruction of such material , return the 
  material in question ( include all copy thereof ) . 
  6.3 the right and responsibility under this section shall survive 
  any termination or cancellation of this agreement for 2 year .
